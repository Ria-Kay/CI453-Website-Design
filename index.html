Intro

The meaning of cyber security is to protect individuals or organisations information system away from cybercrime, by information system it could be any electronic devices such as Personal computer, mobile phones or terminal service. I believe kids like your ages are all curious and interested about internet. After a lone school day some of you like watching funny videos on YouTube or share your happy moments on social medias. However, your parents must have told you to stay away from people you do not know or do not visit any places that you are not feeling comfortable. Well, the online world is not paradise, you will probably still encounter danger if you do not keep your private information safely.
